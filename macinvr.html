About This Game

Welcome to MacIn-VR, the exclusive Social VR experience for Mac!

Browse the Metaverse on your Mac natively! Play in worlds, use your VRM avatars and play with your friends in 3D worlds! No need of Whisky, CrossOver or Wine! MacIn-VR runs solely on the Mac and is meant for the Mac only!

MacIn-VR brings the social VR experience to Mac users without compromises. We've optimized every aspect of the experience for macOS, ensuring that Mac users can finally enjoy VR social platforms without the performance hits and compatibility issues that come with using emulation layers.

Whether you're meeting friends, attending virtual events, or just exploring creative spaces, MacIn-VR provides a seamless gateway to the metaverse specifically designed for your Mac. Join us on our Early Access journey as we continue to expand and enhance the MacIn-VR experience!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What VR hardware is supported?

MacIn-VR is designed to work with Apple Vision Pro as well as standard displays with head tracking via your Mac's camera. Additional VR hardware support will be announced as it becomes available.

What are the system requirements?

MacIn-VR is optimized for Apple Silicon Macs (M1 or newer). While it may run on Intel Macs with dedicated graphics cards, the best experience will be on Apple Silicon hardware.

Will this ever be available for Windows?

No. MacIn-VR is designed exclusively for macOS to provide a truly optimized experience for Mac users without the compromises of cross-platform development.

When will MacIn-VR leave Early Access?

We're currently targeting a full release in late 2025, but this timeline may change as development progresses. Join our Discord for the most up-to-date information.
