Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind NightSightProductions

At NightSightProductions, our team consists of passionate developers, artists, and technologists who share a common vision: to push the boundaries of graphics technology and create immersive gaming experiences.

Together, we combine decades of experience in game development, modding, and graphics programming to bring cutting-edge visual enhancements to both new and classic games.

Leadership

King Vulpes

CEO & Founder

King Vulpes is the visionary leader behind NightSightProductions, with a passion for pushing the boundaries of gaming technology. With extensive experience in graphics programming and a deep understanding of ray tracing technology, they founded NightSightProductions to bring next-generation visual fidelity to beloved classic games.

Under their guidance, our team is working on groundbreaking projects like Portal 2 with RTX and supporting the broader RTX modding community through publishing and technical support.
