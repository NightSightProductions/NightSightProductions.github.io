Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind NightSightProductions

At NightSightProductions, our team consists of passionate developers, artists, and technologists who share a common vision: to push the boundaries of graphics technology and create immersive gaming experiences.

Together, we combine decades of experience in game development, modding, and graphics programming to bring cutting-edge visual enhancements to both new and classic games.

Leadership

Lorenzo Acevedo (King Vulpes)

CEO & Founder

Lorenzo Acevedo, known as King Vulpes, is the visionary leader behind NightSightProductions, with a passion for pushing the boundaries of gaming technology. Coming from a background in game development and community leadership, he established NightSightProductions to create a collaborative platform where talented modders could transform their creative visions into reality.

Chris Medina (Miner 737)

Co-Founder & Co-Owner

Chris Medina, known in the community as Miner 737, is a co-founder of NightSightProductions who helped establish the company's foundation. With his support and vision, NightSightProductions has grown from a small idea into an organization dedicated to enhancing gaming experiences.

Aden Medina (Wispoo)

Co-Founder

Aden Medina, better known as Wispoo, is a co-founder of NightSightProductions who contributed to establishing the company in its early days. His belief in the potential of enhancing gaming experiences helped fuel the initial momentum of the organization.
