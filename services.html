Publishing Process

1

Submission

Submit your Remix project for initial review. We'll evaluate its concept, visual quality, and technical implementation to determine if it's a good fit for our platform.

2

Technical Assessment

Our engineers analyze your mod for performance, stability, and visual fidelity, providing detailed feedback and suggestions for improvement.

3

Refinement

Work with our team to implement suggested improvements and optimizations, ensuring your mod offers the best possible user experience.

4

Marketing Preparation

We prepare promotional materials, screenshots, videos, and project descriptions to effectively showcase your mod to the community.

5

Launch & Support

Your project goes live with a coordinated release across our channels. We provide ongoing technical support and community management after launch.
