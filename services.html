Our Process

1

Consultation & Planning

We begin with a detailed discussion of your project goals, technical constraints, and visual aspirations. Our team works with you to develop a comprehensive plan that aligns with your vision.

2

Technical Assessment

Our engineers analyze the target game or application, identifying the best approach for implementing ray tracing and other visual enhancements while preserving performance.

3

Development & Implementation

We execute the plan, implementing ray tracing, creating or updating materials for PBR workflows, and enhancing geometry as needed. Regular progress updates keep you informed throughout.

4

Testing & Optimization

Rigorous testing across different hardware configurations ensures your project not only looks stunning but runs smoothly. We fine-tune for optimal performance.

5

Delivery & Support

Once everything meets our high standards, we deliver the final product and provide ongoing support to address any issues that may arise post-implementation.
